aBOUT US

Be cooperative.
Be human

Coper.Finance is a global peer-to-peer lending platform built on trust

  • In today’s world, connection is everything — yet when it comes to money, people are often left alone. Coper brings back the human side of finance. It’s a place where anyone, anywhere, can borrow or lend directly, without banks, bureaucracy, or judgment.
  • Coper was created by a team of promising developers forced to leave Russia due to their disagreement with the war unleashed by the Russian government in Ukraine. Facing challenges in finding a new home and bureaucratic hurdles in settling elsewhere, Coper emerged from the collaboration and results of adapting to a new life beyond their homeland. That’s why we designed Coper not as another fintech app, but as a bridge between people — reliable, safe, and borderless.

John Carter

CEO & Co-Founder

Joy Carter

  • Our mission is simple: to make financial help accessible, transparent, and human. Whether it’s funding a student’s dream or helping a small business grow, Coper unites people to support one another — safely, fairly, and with purpose.
